Dear Mr. Grapher,

We write you this letter to inform you that Prince comes with some embedded font families:

Goodfish is a calligraphic serif font in regular, bold, italic, and bold italic.

Effloresce is a delicate serif font with a hand-drawn appearance. Prince can show the regular, bold, italic, and bold italic variants.

Minya Nouvelle is a cartoon typewriter font. If you specify the "fantasy" generic font family in your style sheet, Prince will use Minya Nouvelle. The font is available in regular, bold, italic, and bold italic.

Deftone Stylus is designed to look like hand-painted sign lettering. Prince may use Deftone Stylus if you specify the cursive generic font family.

Unispace is a decorative, squarish monospace font. Available in regular, bold, italic, and bold italic.

All these wonderful fonts have been designed by Ray Larabie.

Sincerely,
Bo Doni
